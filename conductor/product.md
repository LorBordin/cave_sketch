# CaveSketch Product Guide

## Vision
CaveSketch is a lightweight, mobile-friendly application designed to empower cavers to generate professional-quality survey plots and geospatial visualizations directly from TopoDroid DXF exports. It eliminates the need for complex desktop GIS or survey software, making it ideal for field use and rapid data validation during expeditions.

## Target Audience
- **Field Cavers**: Individuals needing quick PDF surveys or location validation while on mobile devices.
- **Expedition Teams**: Groups looking to rapidly merge and visualize survey data from multiple members.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Simplicity & Speed**: Zero-configuration workflow from DXF export to PDF/HTML map.
- **Accessibility**: Browser-based and mobile-optimized for use on any device.
- **Geospatial Context**: Easy georeferencing to see the cave "in place" on satellite imagery.

## Core Features
- **PDF Survey Rendering**: High-quality plan and section views with configurable scale and styling.
- **Interactive Satellite Maps**: Georeferenced overlays on satellite imagery using station coordinates.
- **KML Export**: Export georeferenced cave data for 3D visualization in Google Earth.
- **Multi-Survey Merging**: Support for merging parent and child surveys using station matching, with specialized section protocols like Mirror and Displacement.

## Future Roadmap
- **3D Visualization**: Exporting 3D cave meshes (OBJ).
- **Offline Capabilities**: Improved support for disconnected use in remote areas.
- **Direct TopoDroid Integration**: Synchronizing directly with TopoDroid project files to streamline the export step.
